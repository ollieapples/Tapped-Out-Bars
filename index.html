From the Frontline to the Party

Tapped Out Field Bars was born from a deep respect for the legendary Land Rover chassis. Founded in Solihull, the spiritual home of the brand, we believe that the most rugged engineering in British history deserves a celebratory second mission.

We source original Series and Defender models from all over the UK. Many of our units saw years of service on farms or in the military before arriving at our workshop. We strip them back to the chassis, treating every bolt and panel with care.

Our conversions are a blend of classic grit and refined hospitality. We use reclaimed mahogany for our bartops and custom-turned brass for our taps. Every mobilisation is a mission to make your event unforgettable.
